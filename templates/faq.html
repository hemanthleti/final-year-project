Frequently Asked Questions

Professional guidance for responsible AI skin screening

These answers explain how to use the platform, interpret predictions, and understand the role of clinical follow-up.

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. The platform provides only preliminary AI prediction and should not replace professional clinical evaluation.

What kind of image should be uploaded?

Use a sharp, close, well-lit image focused on the affected skin area.

What does the confidence score mean?

It shows how strongly the model favored its top predicted disease class among the trained options.

Can this replace dermatologist consultation?

No. A certified dermatologist should provide final diagnosis and treatment planning.

What if symptoms worsen quickly?

Seek medical evaluation promptly if symptoms are painful, infected, severe, or rapidly spreading.

Is the site mobile friendly?

Yes. The interface is designed to adapt cleanly across desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.
