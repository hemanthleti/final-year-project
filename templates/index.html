Acne

Usually includes clogged pores, inflammatory lesions, and localized breakouts on oily skin.

Fungal Condition Candidiasis Profile

Candidiasis

Commonly appears in warm or moist areas with redness, irritation, and fungal overgrowth.

Barrier Disorder Eczema Profile
Dryness Itching Irritation

Eczema

Often appears as dry, itchy, irritated, or inflamed skin with barrier-related discomfort.

Chronic Inflammatory Psoriasis Profile
Plaques Scaling Redness

Psoriasis

Can present with thicker scaly plaques, redness, and persistent inflammatory flare patterns.

Vascular Inflammatory Rosacea Profile
Flushing Redness Sensitivity

Rosacea

Often affects the face with persistent redness, visible sensitivity, and flushing-like symptoms.

Fungal Infection Tinea Profile
Rings Edges Spread

Tinea

Frequently appears as ring-like fungal lesions with scaling borders and clearer central areas.

Pigment Disorder Vitiligo Profile
Depigmentation Patches Contrast

Vitiligo

Usually presents as clearly demarcated lighter skin patches due to loss of pigmentation.

Viral Lesion Warts Profile
Raised Rough Localized

Warts

Usually appear as rough, raised, localized growths caused by viral skin infection.
