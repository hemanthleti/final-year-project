Condition overview for the current AI screening classes

These cards provide short explanations for the diseases represented in the current model output.

Acne

Usually includes clogged pores, inflammatory lesions, and localized breakouts on oily skin.

Eczema

Often appears as dry, itchy, irritated, or inflamed skin with barrier-related discomfort.

Psoriasis

Can present with thicker scaly plaques, redness, and persistent inflammatory flare patterns.

Ringworm

Frequently appears as ring-like lesions with a clearer center and more visible outer edge.
